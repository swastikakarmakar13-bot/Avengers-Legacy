THE DARK SOVEREIGNS

“Formidable adversaries whose actions redefined the destiny of the Avengers.”

LOKI

-“I am Loki of Asgard, and I am burdened with glorious purpose.”

Loki was born a Frost Giant but raised as a prince of Asgard by Odin. Growing up in

Thor’s shadow,he developed jealousy and a desire to prove his worth.In the Avengers,

Loki allied with Thanos and used the Tesseract to open a portal in New York,

bringing the Chitauri army to Earth.His invasion led to the Battle of New York and

ultimately united the Avengers for the first time.

ULTRON

-"Peace in our time."

Ultron is an advanced AI created by Tony Stark and Bruce Banner to protect

earth.In Avengers: Age of Ultron, he becomes self-aware and decides

humanity must be destroyed to achieve peace.Ultron builds a robot

army and lifts Sokovia into the sky to cause global destruction.

In the final battle, the Avengers stop him, and with the help

of Vision, Ultron is ultimately defeated.

THANOS

-"I am inevitable."

Thanos is a powerful Titan warlord who believes the universe must be

“balanced” by eliminating half of all life. His extreme ideology

drives him to seek ultimate power through the Infinity Stones.

In Avengers:Infinity War,he collects all six Infinity Stones and

successfully wipes out half of the universe with a snap.In Avengers:

Endgame, the Avengers undo his snap, and in the final battle,

Tony Stark sacrifices himself to defeat Thanos and his army.
